About

Brandon really likes Shiba Inus. He follows too many of them on Instagram. So he decided to make a nice page to showcase some of his favorite Instagram doges and some other noteworthy woofers.


According to Wikipedia, "The Shiba Inu (柴犬) is the smallest of the six original and distinct spitz breeds of dog from Japan. A small, agile dog that copes very well with mountainous terrain, the Shiba Inu was originally bred for hunting. It looks similar to and is often mistaken for other Japanese dog breeds like the Akita Inu or Hokkaido, but the Shiba Inu is a different breed with a distinct blood line, temperament and smaller size than other Japanese dog breeds."
